Root Cause Analysis - Why Performance is Failing

🔴 Traffic Quality Issues

Symptoms:
• 39,167 clicks but only 47 conversions
• 0.12% CVR (should be 0.60%+)
• High impressions, low CTR (1.11%)

Root Causes:
• Wrong keywords attracting browsers, not buyers
• Broad match wasting budget on irrelevant terms
• No negative keywords filtering out poor traffic
• Weak ad copy not pre-qualifying visitors

Impact:
• Paying for 39,000+ clicks from people who won't buy
• CPA inflated to £109.65 (4X breakeven)

🔴 Landing Page Crisis

Symptoms:
• Traffic arriving but not converting
• 99.88% of clicks leave without adding to cart
• High cost per conversion

Root Causes:
• Landing page doesn't match ad message
• Unclear value proposition or call-to-action
• Poor mobile experience (likely issue)
• Missing trust signals & social proof
• Friction in checkout process

Impact:
• 99.88% bounce/exit rate on landing page
• £5,443 spent mostly on wasted traffic

🔴 Account Structure Chaos

Symptoms:
• Low CTR (1.11%) indicating poor relevance
• No campaign segmentation visible
• Budget bleeding across poor performers

Root Causes:
• Single campaign or poorly segmented campaigns
• No brand defense on "cocoon" terms
• High-intent and low-intent mixed together
• No bid strategy optimization per segment

Impact:
• Can't identify winners vs losers
• Budget waste on underperforming segments
• Missing brand traffic opportunities

The £109.65 CPA Crisis Explained

Breakeven CPA: £27.40 (based on 70% close rate, £391.36 breakeven cost per sale, 13% margin)
Current CPA: £109.65 = 300% over breakeven = losing money on every conversion

To hit breakeven, you need either:
1) Reduce CPA from £109.65 to £27.40 (75% reduction) OR
2) Increase CVR from 0.12% to 0.50%+ (317% improvement) OR
3) Combination of both through comprehensive optimization
